THIS IS FLYY

Flyy Studios is an independent, owner-managed Design Studio focused on Lettering and experimental Typography based in Sibiu, Romania.

Since 2022 we create progressive and contemporary type-based designs such as logotypes, titles, posters, covers and merch-designs.

PASSION DRIVEN EXCELLENCE

We’re known for our passion and innovative thinking in our field and left an impression on numerous national and internationally known clients such as Snipes, Zalando, Cheetos, ID Supply, Favela Clothing, Dollars & Isyans, Banger Musik, Maskulin, Shy Glizzy, Honey Dijon, Bhavi and many more.

Further added our work has been showcased by well-known Type-Magazines such as TYPE01 (Issue 06) and Slanted‘s Yearbook of Lettering (2023).
